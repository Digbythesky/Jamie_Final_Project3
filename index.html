what is a solar eclipse?

A solar eclipse is a rare event that occurs when the moon blocks the sun. There are many kinds of eclipses including but not not limited to: Total Solar Eclipse, Annular Solar Eclipse, Partial Solar Eclipse, and Hybrid Solar Eclipse

Create memories with your loved ones!

Life is only so long, its sometimes hard to stay in the present-- but big activities like a solar eclipse can certainly help. Try inviting friends over or have a solar eclipse party!

Engage in festivities

You could make friends or be entertained simply by going out and viewing an eclipse in a public area that has a promotion for it.

Dont forget eyeprotection!

Sight is one of the many senses we as humans have. PLEASE do not look at a solar eclipse without protective eyewhere! It can cause blindness!
